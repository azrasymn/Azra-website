TOP 5 FAVOURITE MOVIES

4. Cruella

Cruella tells the origin story of one of Disney’s most iconic villains — Cruella de Vil — long before the spotted coats and the evil laughs. Set in 1970s London, the film follows Estella, a clever and creative young woman with a passion for fashion and a rebellious streak. Orphaned at a young age and forced to survive on the streets, Estella teams up with two street-smart thieves, making a name for herself through mischief and style. But everything changes when she catches the eye of Baroness von Hellman, a cold, powerful fashion legend. Their complicated relationship and fierce rivalry awaken a darker side of Estella — the bold and unapologetic Cruella. Filled with stunning fashion, punk-rock energy, and unexpected twists, Cruella is a stylish and emotional journey of transformation, revenge, and self-discovery — proving that behind every villain, there’s always a story.

PERSONAL REVIEW

Cruella was such a fun and bold movie. I loved how it showed Estella’s transformation into Cruella in a way that felt emotional yet fierce. But the real star? The fashion. Every outfit was a statement — dramatic, edgy, and full of personality. The designs weren’t just pretty, they told a story. From street style to runway looks, Cruella’s fashion was wild, fearless, and unforgettable.
